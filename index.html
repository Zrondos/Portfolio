Bill Splitter

Going out in a group is fun until the bill comes. “Split” makes sharing a bill easy. Take a photo of your bill and it will be translated to an electronic bill. Drag and drop your friends to the items they ordered or shared. The app then creates separate checks for each of your friends, so everyone knows how much the owe. It’s great for big groups especially when sharing food or drinks.

Olympic Gymnastics Adminstrative Panel

The United States leads the world in Olympic Gymnastics. This administrative panel was developed for the USA Gymnastics Association to easily manage and visualize data regarding gymnasts, coaches, olympic teams and events. Administrators have the ability to register gymnasts and coaches. Administrators can then create teams and events. These gymnasts and coaches can easily be managed by assigning them to Olympic Teams and Olympic Events. The easily to follow design optimizes User Experience making managing an Olympic Sport easy.
